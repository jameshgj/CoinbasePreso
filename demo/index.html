The Gap
Insight apps can't invest. Brokerages can't track spending. No product bridges the gap. Post-Mint, 3.6M active users lost their budgeting tool with no clear successor.
Why This Matters
Robinhood doesn't have an acquisition problem—it has an activation problem. The bucket is leaking. Half the user base isn't trading, and the ones who left had no reason to stay.

Why This Works

No new revenue model needed—distribution is the missing layer

The Economics Are Already There
Trading, Gold, staking, and interchange already exist. The problem isn't that these products don't work—it's that 14M people aren't engaging with any of it. Spending insights is the distribution layer that unlocks the monetization already built.
Market Evidence
RH CPO: "next big step is tackling spending" Post-Mint: no insight→action exists $0 CAC for dormant reactivation
The Competitive Advantage
Robinhood owns the full financial identity: checking accounts, credit products, investments, crypto. No other fintech has this combination. Only Robinhood can make spending insights lead to crypto the way the demo shows.
